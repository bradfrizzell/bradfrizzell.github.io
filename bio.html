Brad Frizzell's Bio

Brad Frizzell is a Senior in Computer Engineering at Tufts University. Brad Frizzell's work in ECE includes many diverse projects. For his Senior Design project, Brad and his teammate Cornell Wilson are working to design and implement a 3-Dimensional camera system for capturing real world measurement data.

In addition to his academic pursuits, Brad is also an avid comedian. He is now in his second year as President of The Tufts Stand Up Comedy Collective. Brad is also a regular writer and performer with Tufts' award winning sketch comedy troupe, Major: Undecided.

Upon completing his Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, Brad Frizzell will be staying for another year at Tufts to earn a Masters in Computer Science. After that, Brad hopes to one day start his own entrepreneurial endeavor.
